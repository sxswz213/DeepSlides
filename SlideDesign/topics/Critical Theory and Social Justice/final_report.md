This philosophical framework aims to analyze and challenge the power structures that shape society, advocating for social justice and the emancipation of marginalized groups. Central to critical theory is the understanding that knowledge and truth are influenced by power dynamics, which often privilege certain groups while oppressing others.\n\nThe theory critiques traditional ideologies and social structures, arguing that they perpetuate inequality and injustice. It encourages a thorough examination of cultural narratives and societal norms to uncover the mechanisms that sustain oppression. By integrating insights from various disciplines, including sociology, psychology, and philosophy, critical theory offers a comprehensive approach to understanding and transforming societal conditions.

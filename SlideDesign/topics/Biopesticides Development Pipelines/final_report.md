This initiative aims to develop environmentally sustainable alternatives to synthetic pesticides, which are often harmful to human health and the ecosystem. icipe's Arthropod Pathology Unit (APU) has been pivotal in this research, leveraging a vast repository of arthropod pathogens and innovative methodologies for product development.\n\nAmong the key microbial strains developed are *Metarhizium anisopliae* strains, which have shown significant efficacy against various agricultural pests, including the fall armyworm (*Spodoptera frugiperda*).

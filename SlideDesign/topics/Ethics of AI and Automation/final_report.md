This principle fosters trust and enables users to scrutinize AI outputs effectively. **Accountability** ensures that organizations are responsible for the outcomes of their AI systems, promoting ethical practices in their development and use.\n\n**Fairness** addresses the need to eliminate biases in AI algorithms, ensuring equitable treatment for all individuals.

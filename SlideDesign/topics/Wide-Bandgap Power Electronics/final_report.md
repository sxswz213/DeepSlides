This wider bandgap allows both materials to operate at higher voltages and temperatures, resulting in improved efficiency and power density.\n\nSiC is particularly noted for its high thermal conductivity, which enables devices to handle higher power densities and operate reliably in extreme conditions, making it ideal for applications like electric vehicle traction inverters and high-power solar inverters [1][3].

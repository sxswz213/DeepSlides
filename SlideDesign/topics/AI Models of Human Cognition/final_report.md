While AI systems, particularly large language models (LLMs), are designed to process vast amounts of data and generate outputs based on statistical patterns, human cognition is characterized by theory-based causal reasoning. Conversely, humans excel in these areas due to their ability to interpret social cues and adapt their strategies accordingly.\n\nMoreover, while AI can automate specific tasks efficiently, it lacks the nuanced understanding and emotional intelligence that humans bring to complex decision-making processes.

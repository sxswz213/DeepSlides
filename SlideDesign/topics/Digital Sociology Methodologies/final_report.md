These methodologies can be broadly categorized into qualitative and quantitative approaches. In contrast, quantitative methods utilize statistical techniques to analyze numerical data, often gathered through surveys or data scraping from online platforms.\n\nData scraping is a crucial technique in digital sociology, allowing researchers to collect large datasets from social media and other online sources.

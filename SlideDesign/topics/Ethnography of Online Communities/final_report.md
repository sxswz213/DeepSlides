One of the primary concerns is obtaining informed consent from participants. Researchers must ensure that individuals understand the purpose of the study and how their data will be used, while also protecting their anonymity and confidentiality throughout the research process [1][2]. Best practices for online informed consent include using accessible platforms and clear communication to ensure participants are fully informed about their rights [2][4].\n\nPrivacy issues are particularly pronounced in digital spaces, where the lines between public and private can be blurred.

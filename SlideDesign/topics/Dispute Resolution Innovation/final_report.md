The evolution of ODR has been marked by significant milestones, including the establishment of platforms like eBay's dispute resolution system in the late 1990s, which set a precedent for online mediation and arbitration processes.\n\nToday, ODR encompasses a wide range of applications, from consumer disputes to complex commercial conflicts.
